Our Story

Kapehan Carigara was born from a deep appreciation for the rich coffee heritage of the Philippines and a desire to showcase the unique flavors of beans sourced and roasted right here in Eastern Visayas. Founded by local coffee enthusiasts, we meticulously select high-quality beans from nearby farms, ensuring sustainable practices and fair compensation for our farmers.

Our roasting process is an art, carefully controlled to bring out the optimal flavor profiles of each bean. From light and fruity to dark and bold, we offer a range of roasts to satisfy every coffee lover's palate. We are committed to providing not just exceptional coffee, but also a taste of our local culture and the warmth of Carigara.

Visit our cafe in Brgy. San Mateo to experience our coffee firsthand or order online to enjoy Kapehan Carigara wherever you are!
